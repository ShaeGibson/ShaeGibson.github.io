Time-Based Animation

This project was creating a Time-Based motion graphic that
went along with the music. My design was inspired by 2D motion
graphic videos that used a lot of shapes and colour; I added my
own ink marble art works into my video to make it more visually
appealing instead of just using solid colour.

I sketched out different transitions in my journal and worked out
a way to make the shapes work in with the music. The video went a
little slow at the start so I then started my video at 20 seconds
in and movedsome of the work I had done further into my video to
make each second intriguing.



Chobani

This project was to create a short advert for the yoghurt 'Chobani'
The ad was to be appealing for bussiness workers on the go. Chobani's
yoghurts use colours to represent the fruits on the packaging. This is
were the inspiration for the video came from and why the use of colour
flows throughout the video.
