Fringe Festival

My design was originally going to be wild animals dressed in circus,
carnival and gang type clothing which was taking into consideration
the slogan 'Born to be wild' This idea felt to original and obvious
so after doing more research, I came across a freelance artist named
Alex Pardee who creates out of the ordinary artworks.

I was then inspired to use watercolour and flowers in my design after
coming across one of his CD covers. Taking into consideration the theme
of 'Born to be wild' I researched wild flowers and how some people use
gumboots as a vase in their backyards.

This gave me the idea of a old black and white boot with colourful wild
flowers coming out of it, which was inspired by Alex's artworks where
he uses a black and white figure or object and contrasts it with lots
of colourful things surrounding it.

After feedback from the class a lot of people said to carry the flowers
around the boot, making it feel more wild I did this and thought it worked
really well as it made the black and white sketched boot stand out more.
One other requirement was a 'splash of pink' So by using a splash of
watercolour in the background it make the boot pop even more.
